ABOUT

A Centralized, Noise Free Dashboard for Monitoring Defense and Geopolitical Situations.


MOTIVATION

I built this to solve my own frustration. Monitoring geopolitics across X, YouTube, and News sites became too distracting, ads-heavy and filled with algorithmic-bloat. This was my personal offline tool to keep myself informed about latest developements.

Now it's public and open-source, because probably many people might be dealing with the same problem. This platform gives you a snapshot of what's happening, so you only deep-dive into what actually matters.

CONTENT

The website gets updated every 60 minutes, fast enough to stay current, & slow enough to avoid IP blocks from platforms like Twitter, YouTube, and major media outlets. Only the last 48 hours of data is shown, older feed is not stored in any form. The dashboard is meant to be a "pulse", not an "archive".

Since everyone has their own preferred news outlets and analysts, there's a built-in Source Filter so you can tune the feed to what matters to you.

SUPPORT

This is a volunteer-led effort. While the website will always remain free to use, its value is defined by the quality and reach of its data. There are several ways you can help me improve, depending on your interests and skills.

How you can help:

  • Feed Curator: Help us make the feed exhaustive. If we’ve missed a major news outlet, a specific analyst, or a YouTube channel, suggest them by opening a Pull Request or reaching out via Contact.
  • Development: Help me refine the website. Whether it’s optimizing the UI, improving feed parsing, or fixing bugs, your code contributions on GitHub can help make this website faster and more reliable.
  • Donation: If you find this tool valuable, consider making a donation of any amount, to support the creator.
  • Spread the Word: Share the platform with your community or give us a Star on GitHub. Visibility is the best way to ensure the project continues to evolve.
